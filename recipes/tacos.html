Taco Skillets

Description

This taco skillet is the perfect way to treat yourself from dinner in an easy to make and fast recipe. This pairs well with a light beer and chips and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • 1 oz packaged taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Drain the skillet and stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes.
  4. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
